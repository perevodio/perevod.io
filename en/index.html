About

What is Perevod.io?

It's a money transfer web-service based on Siberian Chervonets (SIB) cryptocurrency - sibcoin.org

In order to make a transfer anywhere in the world you would have to know a phone number of recipient and to create a secret word. To make a new tranfer to the same phone it's enough to create a new secret word. Number of transfers to the same phone is not limited.

To make a transfer:

  1. create a secret word and keep it safely, then start a transfer on the web-site;
  2. using exchange service, make a deposit;
  3. provide recipient with the secret word chosen.

Attention: the secret word can not be recovered in case of lose!

To receive a transfer:

  1. use «Receive transfer» option on a exchange service web-site (list of exchange services);
  2. use a reception method (you are selling SIB, receiving a payment to the bank card or to the account) and enter the numer of the card/account;
  3. enter phone number and secret word, confirm the transfer of SIB and wait for the operation to be completed.

Privacy

We have no access to customers' funds and we are unable to keep customers' credentials, and we do not keep IP addresses, phones etc. If you wish you are able to download this web-site and use it offline with no internet access. SSL is used as well in order to secure customers' sensitive data.

SMS-nofitifaction on SIB operations

External system is used by this web-service for SMS notifications. The phone number will be provided to that system only upon customer's choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

– How do I come up with a secure secret word?
The secret word should consist of upper- and lowercase letters, digits and extra characters like «-», @ etc.

- How do I check the balance of the transfer?
Enter phone number and the secret word, the balance will be displayed.

- I'm entering the phone number and the secret word, but the balance is zero, although it should not have been so. What I should I do?
Verify the phone number and the secret word and try again with correct ones.

– What should I do if the exchange service didn't deposit my funds?
Contact the customers support of the exchange service and provide the evidence of your payment.

– What can I do if my secret word has been compromised?
Make a new transfer as soon as possible using another secret word and send all your SIBs to the new wallet. The destination address is displayed under the QR code and you may use «Spend» button for transfer.

- How can I transmit my secret word to the recipient to avoid it's compromise?
It's recommended to use cryptographic communications. If you do not use those, you may transmit your secret word in parts using different communications (e.g. one part using e-mail and another over phone call)

- How do I know the exact exchange rate if I do receive another currency?
The rates are set by exchange services and those are too volatile. Try looking up SIB/Currency1 and SIB/Currency2 rates.

How to get in list of exchangers at perevod.io?

Online exchange services as well as offiline exchangers are welcome to participate. In order to get in list of approved exchangers here you would have to:

  • Announce your exchange service at Sibcoin community forums (http://sibcoin.org/community) and wait for testing from respected participants to be completed.
  • Deposit a $1000 (in SIB) to the Sibcoin development fund http://forum-nso.ru/threads/2023/. The deposit would be used in case of fraud to satisfy the suffered customers.

Integration

To complete the customer's transfer you have to make your system able to accept the following parameters:

  • from=CURRENCY_FROM_NAME (payment currentcy, i.e. QIWI_RUR)
  • to=SIB (destination currency = SIB)
  • account=SdxrquqyV82sphxjqBELYa7DUNuXiMS2wk (destination address where the SIB would be sent after the purchase)
  • wth_currency=SIB (currency for SMS-notification to be paid optionally)
  • wth_address=SXZj3geo1UqATtPFJsPLVRcRvPJycyJhF3 (SIB address to pay for SMS-notification)
  • wth_value=1 (SIB value for SMS-notification service)
  • wth_details=SMS-confirmation (SMS-notification service description to be displayed to the customer)

Example:
https://domain.com/?from=CURRENCY_FROM_NAME&to=SIB&start=1&account=SdxrquqyV82sphxjqBELYa7DUNuXiMS2wk&wth_currency=SIB&wth_address=SXZj3geo1UqATtPFJsPLVRcRvPJycyJhF3&wth_value=1&wth_details=SMS-confirmation

The customer will be redirected from the wallet by the link with the corresponding parameters, then you get a payment in usual currency and you send SIB to the given address with SMS-notification service cost deducted (separate payment, if ordered by the customer).

To receive the transfer from the customer wishing to make an exchange «SIB»usual currency» in your system you have to open an iframe or redirect the customer to the link looking like https://perevod.io/?sur=sibcoinaddress assuming sibcoinaddress as your address where you expect the customer to send SIB.

Example:
<iframe src="https://perevod.io/?sur=SURbRbWkruAkcwLaZnEcX9hByn7WHotECU">perevod.io is loading...</iframe>

The customer should sign and send a transfer to the address provider on her/his own. You would only have to wait for SIB to be deposited and then send the regular currency transfer to the customer.
